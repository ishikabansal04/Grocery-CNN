Red beets has an earthy and sweet taste and is often eaten on the side, either cooked or preserved. It is also common to make Borstj, red beet soup, but the beet can also be shredded as raw food. Red beets are slightly sensitive to heat, frostbites and dehydration and should therefore be kept cool.
Vitamins are Fiber, Vitamin B9 and Vitamin C.
Minerals are Manganese, Potassium and Iron.
